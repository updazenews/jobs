About us

Sebenza Portal is a dedicated job listing platform and a proud sub-division of Updaze Digital Solutions.
Our core mission is simple:
to connect employers with job seekers by providing a reliable, easy-to-use platform where opportunities are shared openly and transparently. Sebenza Portal allows companies, organisations, and institutions to post:

  • Job vacancies
  • Internships
  • Learnerships
  • Bursaries

We are not a recruitment agency or a hiring agency. We do not shortlist candidates, conduct interviews, or make hiring decisions. Instead, we serve as a digital bridge, giving employers a space to publish opportunities and giving job seekers direct access to verified listings from multiple industries.
As part of Updaze Digital Solutions, Sebenza Portal is built on a technology-driven foundation that prioritises:

  • Accessibility and ease of use
  • Accurate and timely job information
  • A professional and transparent job-posting environment

Our platform is designed to support career growth, skills development, and employment access by making opportunities more visible to a wider audience.
At Sebenza Portal, we believe opportunity should be accessible — and finding the next step in your career shouldn’t be complicated.
